Get started

Born in Mumbai in the late 1800s, Dabbawalas (dabba meaning ‘box’ in Hindi) have kept cities fed for over a hundred years. It began as a stacked dinner delivered by bike or railway train to neighbourhood workers, who would enjoy their meal from top drawer to bottom and give back the containers on the delivery person's return journey.

Our Mission

As busy, time-strapped mums, my friend Anshu and I knew the drill of ordering takeaway all too well. Yet, the whole experience, from the quality of the food to the packaging and delivery, left us longing for something more. In 2018, fuelled by our love for simple, wholesome meals and a profound commitment to sustainability, we embarked on a mission. And so, DabbaDrop was born - our meals are made using cherished family recipes from South Asia and arrive in Mumbai's finest dabba tins, collected on the next delivery, sterilised, refilled, and reused again and again.

Join us on our journey, and let's change the way we experience takeaway together.

Thanks,
Renee
